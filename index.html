About Me

Your Photo Here

"I don't stay in one lane.
I build the road."

I'm Chandu Shree — a multidisciplinary creator who refuses to be boxed in. I write code and I write stories. I research data and I make art. I'm building a business while building myself.

My work is fuelled by a simple belief: that the most interesting things happen at the intersection of disciplines. Engineering informs my aesthetics. Art sharpens my logic. Writing gives everything else a soul.

Right now I'm channeling everything into Kahaani Karamel — a venture that's deeply personal and entirely mine. More on that below.
